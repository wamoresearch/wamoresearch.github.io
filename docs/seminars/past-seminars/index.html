Adelaide-Nottingham Alliance Colloquium

  • When? 17th September 2024

  • Where? Room A17, School of Mathematical Sciences, University Park, NG7 2RD

  • Organizers: Tristan Lawrie, Gregor Tanner

This is a one-day workshop mainly based on Wave Modelling to be held in the School of Mathematical Sciences, Room A17 on the 17th September 2024 as part of the Adelaide-Nottingham Alliance programme. Everybody is welcome to attend.
